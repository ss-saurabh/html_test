When did Taco first appear in the india?

Taco, the Mexican dish, gained popularity in India around the early 2000. It started appearing in major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore primarily through the influence of international fast food chains like Taco Bell and local restaurants experimenting with fusion cuisines. Over time, tacos have become more widely available across India, both in restaurants and as a homemade dish. (sourse: History of the taco: A dish that evolved across borders)
